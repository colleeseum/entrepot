Why Alfred?

Purpose-built storage only 45 minutes east of Ottawa / 60 minutes west of Montreal

Located in Alfred, Ontario with multiple indoor barns for RVs, cars and sleds, plus drive-through lanes and an outdoor yard poured in concrete. The same family that runs Colle Farm oversees every check-in.
