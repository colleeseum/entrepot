Why Alfred?

Storage rules

Simple guardrails keep everyone on schedule

Winter key dates

  • Drop-off window: 17 October – 1 November 2025.
  • Pick-up deadline: 26 April 2026 ($5/day late fee).
  • No gasoline cans or propane tanks in stored vehicles.

Summer key dates

  • Season: 3 May – 10 October 2025.
  • Pick-up times confirmed 7 days ahead.
  • Battery disconnect service included for cars.

Access & maintenance

  • Vehicles remain locked; access during storage triggers a handling fee.
  • Notify us 48 hours before winter drop-offs and 7 days before summer pickups.
  • Tell us if a vehicle leaks oil so we can prep drip trays.
